This page gathers the compliance and legal framing for Pipeline. Nothing on this page is legal advice. Consult your own counsel before participating.

## Compliance screening

Pipeline does not collect identity documents, corporate paperwork, or accreditation declarations from lenders. Compliance is enforced by KYT (Know Your Transaction) screening on every deposit, on standalone address enrolments, and on scheduled passive re-screening. The transfer whitelist has a 90-day freshness window. The legal framework that governs this approach is `[Framework: TBD]`. See [lender onboarding](/lenders/onboarding/) for the full flow.
