System Context
All components. Off-chain custody and ops on the left, on-chain contracts on the right, governance Safes below.
Governance
Three Safes gate the AccessManager: slow writes via Admin, risk actions via Risk Council, instant defensive cancels via Guardian.

Admin Safe · 3/5

48 h delay on all writes

  • Role grants and revocations
  • Selector → role mappings
  • Per-target admin delays
  • Rate limits and venue lists
  • System-whitelist adds
  • Contract upgrades

Risk Council · 3/5

24 h delay on writes

  • Mark loan default on LoanRegistry
  • Trigger ShutdownController
  • Adjust recovery rate (up only)
  • Narrow scope — credit and wind-down

Guardian · 2/5

instant · defensive only

  • Pause any managed contract
  • Cancel any scheduled op
  • Call EmergencyRevoker.revokeAll
  • Cannot grant roles or move funds
Deposit → Mint
LP deposits USDC via DepositManager; PLUSD is minted 1:1 atomically. No off-chain signer on the deposit leg — the on-chain USDC transfer is the attestation.
Stake / Unstake
LP deposits PLUSD into the sPLUSD ERC-4626 vault and later redeems back to PLUSD. Stake reverts during shutdown; unstake stays open as the exit path to PLUSD → `redeemInShutdown`.
Yield Accretion
Borrower repays Capital Wallet; Bridge and custodian co-sign an EIP-712 YieldAttestation; PLUSD.yieldMint verifies both on-chain and mints into sPLUSD vault.
Withdraw → Settle
LP redeems PLUSD for USDC via FIFO queue. Bridge triggers funding; USDC flows from Capital Wallet to the queue.
Loan Lifecycle
Soulbound loan NFT: mint, amend, default, close. Registry is audit trail only — holds no capital.
Shutdown
Terminal one-way wind-down. Holders pull USDC from RecoveryPool at a fixed rate; Trustee tops up as capital returns; rate ratchets up only.
Incident → RevokeAll
Bridge or Trustee key compromise: pause cascade, atomic role revocation, key rotation.
